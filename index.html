Welcome to Steam Power Academy

Welcome to Steam Power Academy where we welcome folks from all walks of life to join in our pursuit of the exquisitries and fineries of Eorzea. Craft your dreams together denizens and friends and never hesitate to ask for help. Full Steam Ahead!!! O:

A safe place for all where everyone is respected for their specific talents. This FC is member owned and promises trust and value on all members.

Formed on 06/10/2017 a group of friends, united by a dream, embarked on a journey to form a solid foundation for a family friendly FC that people can call home. We are committed to sustainability and long lasting friendships. Take heart dear friends knowing that your contributions, whether its your fun and friendly personality, if you are great at your class, love crafting and gathering and helping others, are all great and valued equally. We are here to help eachother and to create an environment where everyone feels respected and comfortable. We hope you enjoy being here and look forward to many great times and adventures with you!
